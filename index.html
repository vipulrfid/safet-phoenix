Disclaimer. SafeT predicts a relative risk tier (Low / Moderate / High) for a given location and time, based on historical Phoenix PD crime patterns and U.S. Census population. It does not forecast specific incidents, and predictions are not real-time — the underlying data is refreshed periodically, not live. The model excludes calls outside Phoenix PD's jurisdiction (Scottsdale, Tempe, Mesa, Chandler, Gilbert, Glendale, Peoria, etc. publish their own feeds separately). Time-based test accuracy is the honest "predict 2024-2025 from 2021-2023" number — measurably better than random, but bounded by the year-to-year non-stationarity of crime patterns. Do not use as the sole input for safety decisions.
